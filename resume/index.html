About Me

My name is Khushboo Rathore and I am a third-year Journalism and Information Sciences student, also earning a University Honors citation, at University of Maryland, College Park. I have experience in data-based journalism, feature writing, beat coverage, and entertainment coverage.
Journalism has been my passion for a long time. I was on my high school newspaper, Montgomery Blair High School's Silver Chips , and am currently a Diversions writer for my university's newspaper, The Diamondback and the summer Editorial Intern at The Frederick News Post.
Outside of journalism, I enjoy reading and playing a variety of video games. I am also a volunteer puppy raiser for the Guide Dog Foundation and America's Vet Dogs.
